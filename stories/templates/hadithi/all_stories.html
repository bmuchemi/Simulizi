Few Words from the author

My name is Lofty. I'm a stories enthusiast. I love reading books, especially books by African authors but most importantly I write Short stories too, sprinkled with some humor that will put a smile on your face. You have a bad day? Reach out to Lofty and catch some funny stories. Mostly, I write about my daily or weekly activities, about people I meet, about music, about football and many more things. Ooh also, I'm a poet. I write poems, about reciting them? Not very sure. The voice is wanting... but let's see.

Poems

Most of my poems are about love,yes erotic love. But I don't limit myself to that. You could find any kind of poem here. Honestly, I can't say I have read so much poetry and I haven't indulged in it as I have in stories but I write them nonetheless.

Story

Like I mentioned above, of poetry and stories, I love the latter and I do it with so much ease. I cannot claim to have read so many books but I'm learning while writing.
